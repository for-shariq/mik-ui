Muharam in Kashmir | Disseminating the message of Karbala

The Holy Prophet (S.a.w.a) said: Surely, there exists in the hearts of the Mu'mineen, with respect to the martyrdom of Husayn (A.S.), a heat that never subsides.

Mustadrak al‑Wasail vol 10 pg. 318

Muharram In Kashmir is an initiative based on digitizing the audio and video of Kashmiri old/new nauha, marsiyah, video's etc.

The team includes a group of volunteers and has no association with any group or political entity.

The vision of the initiative is to create a Digital Islamic Library for Kashmir. Please write us for you suggestions at: feedback.mik@gmail.com
